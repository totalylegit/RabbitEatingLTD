Food facts of the Day

Did you know that carrots are not only delicious but also packed with vitamins and minerals?

They are a great source of beta-carotene, which is converted into vitamin A in the body, essential for good vision and immune function.

Carrots also contain antioxidants and fiber, making them a healthy addition to your diet.
